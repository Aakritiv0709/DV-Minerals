About Us

Established in the year 2020 in Madhya Pradesh , we " DV Minerals and Associate " is an Individual Proprietor firm, engaged as a Manufacturer and Wholesaler , Trader of Copper Earthing Wire , Audio Cable Loudspeaker Speaker Cable.We take pride in being the first wire and cable company in India to have such a diverse and extensive range of products. We are one of the leading companies in electrical and copper industry & our journey to success as well as leadership has been complimented with a desire to adapt with new innovations and technology. We are accredited with the highest number of product and system certifications, with a continuous pursuit of evolving technology, we are determined of achieving even greater success.
